CHICAGO OUTFIT ROLLER DERBY

From Chicago Outfit Roller Derby website... Chicago's Derby Little Secret The Chicago Outfit was founded in the fall of 2007. We are a democratic roller derby league run by the skaters, for the skaters. We are an all-female flat track roller derby league based in the city of Chicago. Our goal is to create a positive female community dedicated to contributing charitably to other non-profit organizations. We, as skaters, strive to develop the skills necessary to compete within the Women's Flat Track Derby Association (WFTDA). As of June 2017, 2010, we are proud members of WFTDA. The Chicago Outfit represents roller derby internationally with class, sass, and dignity. For more information: http://www.chicagooutfitrollerderby.com

    • HOUSING FORWARD - ending homelessness

    From Housing Forward website... Housing Forward... ending homelessness The mission of Housing Forward is to transition people from housing crisis to housing stability. Housing Forward is the only social service agency in west Cook County with a comprehensive "housing & services" solution for transitioning individuals and families out of homelessness. Over the past 25 years, we have developed a unique, integrated approach that to date has transformed the lives of more than 15,000 people. Our solution is comprehensive and long-term, not just a quick fix, which benefits Housing Forward's clients and also the communities in which we operate. For more information: http://www.housingforward.org
